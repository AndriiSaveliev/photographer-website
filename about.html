About me

I’m Andrii. I like clean, natural images and a simple client experience. This site is a student project, but I’m practicing real-world standards: accessibility, privacy, and honest communication.

Ethics & privacy

I do not sell personal data. The contact form collects only what is needed to reply (name, email, message). Photos are used in portfolio only with client permission.

Accessibility

I use headings (H1/H2), readable font sizes, keyboard-friendly navigation, and meaningful alt text. This helps users with screen readers and improves overall usability.
